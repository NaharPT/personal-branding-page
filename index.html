I'm Nuno Galante Valério -- a Portuguese pharmacist turned innovation leader, currently heading R&D Quality Technology & Data at Merck Healthcare in Germany, where I drive digital transformation, GenAI adoption, and compliant quality systems for global R&D.

With 20+ years across community pharmacy, health economics, and pharmaceutical quality, I've built a career on making complex systems more human. I own the eQMS roadmap (Veeva Vault QMS), lead cross-functional digital strategy, and push for data-driven quality intelligence.

Outside the corporate world, I'm a builder at heart. I use AI to ship tools, write books, create content, and design systems for everything from personal finance to health tracking. Father of three. Lifelong learner. Endlessly curious.
